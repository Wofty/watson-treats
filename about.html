Our Mission

Enriching lives through sweet moments, Watson Treats strives to be a catalyst for joy and connection in the heart of Liverpool. With our delectable range of handcrafted treats, we aim to create unforgettable experiences that bring people together. Whether it's a birthday, wedding, or any special occasion, we believe that every celebration deserves a touch of sweetness!

Our Vision

We envision a community where the aroma of freshly baked treats intertwines with shared laughter, creating a haven where every visit to Watson Treats is a celebration of life's simple pleasures.

What We Do

We specialise in crafting artisanal cakes using time‐honoured techniques fused with innovative twists. Our focus extends to curating exceptional tea experiences, indulgent cakes, and bespoke catering, ensuring each moment is infused with the finest flavours and a touch of culinary artistry.
